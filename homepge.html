khalighat NEW

A popular religious attraction of Kolkata, Kalighat Kali Temple is considered as one of the 52 Shakti Peethas of India. Kalighat is said to be the site where the toes of the right foot of Sati fell.

Iskon TempleNEW

Mayapur is believed as the birth place of Chaitanya Mahaprabhu It is the headquarters of ISKCON Temples in India. Srila Prabhupada's (founder of ISKCON) memorial is the main attraction in Mayapur. This samadhi mandir gets thousands of visitors year round

Victorial Memorial

The Victoria Memorial is a large marble building in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, which was built between 1906 and 1921. It is dedicated to the memory of Queen Victoria, then Empress of India, and is now a museum under the auspices of the Ministry of Culture. The memorial lies on the Maidan (grounds) by the bank of the Hooghly River
